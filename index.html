How It Works

Four steps.
One seamless guest experience.

1
Guest Messages
Your guest texts the hotel WhatsApp number — any language, any hour. No app download, no registration. Just a message.
2
AI Understands
The concierge reads intent instantly — booking request, room service, local recommendation, complaint — and crafts a perfect, personal reply.
3
Partners Alerted
The right partner — taxi, restaurant, spa — gets a WhatsApp alert automatically and confirms in one tap. No calls, no coordination.
4
Hotel Earns
Every confirmed booking earns your hotel a commission, tracked automatically and reconciled monthly. Your team gets the credit. The platform handles the work.
