How It Works

Four steps.
One seamless guest experience.

1
Guest Messages
Guest texts your hotel's WhatsApp number. Any language, any time of day or night.
2
AI Understands
Our AI instantly reads intent — booking request, room service, local recommendation, complaint — and crafts a perfect reply.
3
Partners Alerted
Taxi, restaurant, spa — the right partner gets a WhatsApp alert and confirms in one tap: ✅ ❌.
4
Hotel Earns
Every confirmed booking earns your hotel a commission, tracked automatically and settled monthly.
